Our Story

Bean Board was born from a passion for great coffee and meaningful connections. Founded in 2012, we began as a small cart at the local farmers market, serving freshly roasted coffee to our community.

As our reputation for quality and service grew, we opened our first brick-and-mortar location in 2015. Today, Bean Board stands as a testament to our commitment to craft, community, and coffee culture.

Our name "Bean Board" represents our approach to coffee: carefully selecting each bean, understanding its journey from seed to cup, and presenting a diverse board of flavor profiles for our customers to experience.
